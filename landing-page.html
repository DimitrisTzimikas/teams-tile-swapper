Features

🎯

Focal point picker

Click the preview to choose which part of your image stays centered on the tile.

✨

Blurred background fill

In Fit mode, a blurred copy of the image fills the tile so nothing looks letterboxed.

📺

Leaves screens alone

Only participant tiles are swapped. Active shared screens and live video stay untouched.

🔒

Private and local

No analytics, no tracking. Your custom image is stored in your browser only.

Get started in 30 seconds

1
Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store
2
Open Microsoft Teams on the web and join a meeting
3
Click the extension icon in the toolbar
4
Pick Custom, Cats, or Dogs — the tiles update instantly
